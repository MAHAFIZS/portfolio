About Me

Welcome! I'm M A HAFIZ, an enthusiastic Engineer specializing in Biomedical Engineering and Assistive Intelligence Robotics. My work is driven by a passion to create seamless interaction between humans and machines through innovative, human-centered designs.

I’m skilled in Python, MATLAB, C++, C#, ROS, ROS2, OpenCV, and simulation platforms like MuJoCo and Gazebo. I develop intuitive control systems, real-time robotic applications, and intelligent perception solutions.

As part of my working student job at FAPS – Institute for Factory Automation and Production, FAU Erlangen-Nürnberg, I developed a complete web-based Acceptance Model Generator for evaluating Human-Robot Interaction (HRI). The app allows users to configure robot types, task contexts, and team dynamics, and dynamically generates acceptance constructs using an Excel-connected backend.

In September 2023, I completed a Summer Internship at the EXCITE Zurich Summer School on Biomedical Imaging, hosted by ETH Zurich and the University of Zurich. The program provided hands-on experience in biomedical imaging, and I received an official certificate upon successful completion.

My M.Sc. thesis focused on gesture-based intuitive control of the Franka Emika Panda robot using EMG signals. The system interprets human intent for tasks like peg-in-hole insertion and screwing, using a custom MuJoCo simulation and real-time gesture switching.

I hold a B.Sc. in Electrical & Electronic Engineering from CUET and have completed my M.Sc. coursework in Medical Engineering at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg, with a specialization in Medical Image and Data Processing.
